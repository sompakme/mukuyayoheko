The Cast Of Netflix's 'The Adam Project,' Ranked By Net Worth

September 11, 2024
blog

The Sci-Fi/Drama movie, The Adam Project, features a star-studded cast. You might be wondering what The Adam Project is, and we're here to tell you.

The Adam Project, according to its official description, is about a time-traveling fighter pilot, Adam Reed, who accidentally crash lands in 2022, and then teams up with his 12-year-old self and his late father on a mission to save the future. The movie will land on Netflix on March 11, 2022.

Directed by Shawn Levy (Night at the Museum, Free Guy), The Adam Project stars Ryan Reynolds, Zoe Saldana, Mark Ruffalo, Jennifer Garner and more. With this movie being classified as a drama, this is a new role for Reynolds, as he is usually in comedies, but it seems like this will prove how versatile he is.

Here's the cast of The Adam Project, ranked by net worth.

9 Walker Scobell - Unknown Net Worth

Walker Scobell plays a young Adam Reed, who meets up with his older self to try and stop time travel. The Adam Project is his first role in a movie or TV show and his net worth is not known. However, he is set to star in the upcoming movie, Secret Headquarters, so his net worth should increase drastically. At only 13-years-old, his career is just taking off.

8 Lucie Guest - $1 - $5 Million

Lucie Guest is only labeled as actress in The Adam Project, so it is not clear on the role she plays. Guest is best known for acting, directing and writing movies and TV shows including The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina, Hypnotic, iZombie and more. Her net worth is not publicly known, but some websites put her between $1 to $5 million.

7 Braxton Bjerken - $1 - $5 Million

Braxton Bjerken plays Ray Dollarhyde. Not much is known about the character yet. His net worth is estimated to be between $1 to $5 million, however that is not confirmed. He is known for starring in The House with a Clock in its Walls, Thunder Force and Good Girls.

6 Alex Mallari Jr. - $2 Million

Alex Mallari Jr. plays a character called Christos. His net worth is not officially confirmed, but many websites put him at a $2 million net worth. Mallari Jr. is known for his work in TV including the series Shadowhunters and Ginny & Georgia. He has starred in other film and TV roles, but those two are his biggest, which is why the 34-year-old's net worth is still building.

5 Catherine Keener - $8 Million

Catherine Keener's net worth is estimated to be $8 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. In The Adam Project, she plays an undisclosed character, but she is listed in the IMDb page for the movie. The Academy Award nominee has starred in movies such as Capote, Being John Malkovich, The 40-Year-Old Virgin, Get Out, Into The Wild and more.

4 Zoe Saldaña - $35 Million

Zoe Saldaña is set to play the character of Laura in film. Her goal is to stop time travel from being invented at all. According to CelebrityNetWorth, her estimated net worth is $35 million. She has earned most of that money through her acting career, where she has starred in films such as Guardians of the Galaxy and Avengers, Pirates of the Caribbean, Star Trek and Avatar. Being in some of the highest-grossing movies of all-time has only helped Zoe Saldaña's net worth.

3 Mark Ruffalo - $35 Million

Mark Ruffalo plays Louis Reed, Adam Reed's father. According to Celebrity Net Worth, his net worth is estimated to be $35 million. He has acted in, directed, produced and written many movies, including 13 Going on 30, many Marvel movies, The Kids Are Alright, Where The Wild Things Are, and more. He earned an estimated $2 to $3 million to portray the Incredible Hulk in the Marvel movies, earning him his biggest paycheck.

2 Jennifer Garner - $80 Million

Jennifer Garner's net worth is said to be $80 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. In The Adam Project, Garner plays Ellie Reed, Adam Reed's mom. She is also known for her roles in many films including 13 Going on 30 (in which she also stars with Ruffalo), Ghost of Girlfriends Past, Valentine's Day, Love, Simon, Yes Day and many more.

But aside from her acting and producing roles, the 49-year-old has earned some of her money through brand endorsements, including Capital One and Neutrogena. She also co-founded her own business, "Once Upon a Farm," which sells organic baby food.

1 Ryan Reynolds - $150 Million

Ryan Reynolds plays the main character, Adam Reed (from the future), who travels to the past to save the future. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Reynolds' net worth is $150 million. From his time in Deadpool and many other movies, including The Proposal, Free Guy, Ryan Reynolds has racked up an impressive net worth for a 45-year-old. Besides acting in movies, Reynolds has been featured in commercials and is also an ambassador for Aviation American Gin, where he owns an unspecified minority ownership stake. This net worth could also be shared with his wife, actress Blake Lively.
