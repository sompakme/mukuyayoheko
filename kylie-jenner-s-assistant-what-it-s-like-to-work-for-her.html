Kylie Jenners Assistant: What Its Like to Work For Her

September 07, 2024
blog

Not your average 9 to 5. Kylie Jenner’s personal assistant Victoria Villarroel revealed what it’s like to work for the Lip Kit creator via Jenner’s website on Monday, December 19.

“Five years ago, I started working at her mom’s company, Jenner Communications, and I would see Kylie there all the time 'cause it was her mom’s company,” Villarroel, 24, shared in a video posted to Jenner’s app, revealing she was hired by the teenage mogul about a year ago. “They just took me,” she said. “It’s work but it’s fun.”

While the pair work together, they’re also super close pals.
