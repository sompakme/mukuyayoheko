    A Favorite Recipe from Michelle Duggar: Chili Frito Pie

    In an interview, Michelle Duggar shared one of her favorite recipes that she makes often for her large family. It’s called Chili Frito Pie, and it’s an easy and delicious dinner option. The recipe consists of Mexican-style chili beans, Fritos™, shredded cheddar cheese, and sour cream. Michelle emphasizes that it’s quick to prepare and nutritious, making it a hit with her children.

    Here’s the recipe for you to try at home:

  • In a large skillet, heat the Mexican-style chili beans over medium heat until warmed through.
  • While the beans are heating, crush a handful of Fritos™ and spread them on a serving plate.
  • Once the beans are warmed, pour them over the crushed Fritos™.
  • Top the chili beans with a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese.
  • Add a dollop of sour cream on top of the cheese.
  • Optional: You can also add additional toppings such as diced onions, jalapenos, or sliced olives.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
