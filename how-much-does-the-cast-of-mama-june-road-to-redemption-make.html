How Much Does Mama June Get Paid For Mama June: Road To Redemption?

Since Mama June: Road to Redemption is produced for We TV, a channel some people have never heard of, the show likely doesn't have a huge budget. On the other hand, since Mama June has been famous for many years, it seems unlikely that she would star in the show for peanuts.

Fortunately for anyone who wants to know how much Mama June gets paid for her current reality TV show, they don't have to take a shot in the dark about that figure. The reason for that is a legal filing revealed a lot of information about Mama June's income.

Unfortunately for everyone involved, there is a good reason why the Thompson family has been tabloid fodder for years. Ever since they have been in the spotlight, the Thompson family saga has taken some truly shock twists and turns.

One of the most recent revelations about the Thompson family is that Mama June has lost custody of her famous daughter Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thomson. Instead, Alana's big sister Lauryn "Pumpkin" Efird now has full custody of Honey Boo Boo.

As a result of that custody arrangement, a court had to decide how much money in child support Mama June was going to pay Pumpkin. To decide on that figure, the court needed to find out how much money Mama June makes.

In June 2022, People was able to attain the legal paperwork about the custody agreement. As a result, People revealed that Mama June pays Pumpkin $800 a month in child support. More importantly for the purposes of this article, People also revealed how much money Mama June makes monthly.

According to the legal filings, Mama June's "earns approximately $25,000 per month". The legal paperwork also stipulates that most of that income is dependent on the money she makes from Mama June: Road to Redemption's "production and endorsements".

Furthermore, the legal filings stipulate that the only income that Mama June that isn't related to Mama June: Road to Redemption is the $1,015.00 she gets per month for disability.

With all of that in mind, Mama June reportedly makes roughly $24,000 per month from Mama June: Road to Redemption. That said, it has to be noted that money comes from a mixture of her TV salary and endorsement deals she has because of the show.
