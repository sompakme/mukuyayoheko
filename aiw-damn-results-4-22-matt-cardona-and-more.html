AIW Damn! Results (4/22): Matt Cardona And More

August 25, 2024
blog

On April 22, Absolute Intense Wrestling held its AIW Damn! event on April 22 from the Tadmor Shrine in Akron, OH. Matt Cardona, Steph De Lander, Tom Lawlor, and other stars competed at the show, which aired on FITE TV. 

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Austin James def. Tyson Riggs
  • Money Shot (Zach Nystrom & Elijah Dean) def. Bulking Season (Chuck Stone & Arthur McArthur)
  • Steph De Lander def. Ziggy Haim
  • The BitcoinboiZ (Mikey Montgomery & Eric Taylor) (with The Duke) (c) def. Trademark Name Army (Wes Barkley & Swoggle)
  • Dominic Garrini def. Fred Rosser
  •  Youthanazia (Matt Cross & Josh Prohibition) def. The Philly Marino Experience (Philly Collins & Marino Tenaglia)
  • Derek Dillinger (with Ziggy Haim) (c) def. Chase Oliver and Tom Lawlor and Shaw Mason
  • Sam Holloway def. Kaplan
  • Matt Cardona (with Bill Alfonso & Steph De Lander) (c) def. Joshua Bishop
