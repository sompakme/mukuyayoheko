About The Fidget Game

The Fidget Game is a captivating and interactive learning tool designed to enhance reading skills in children from Pre-K to Grade 3. By incorporating elements of play and exploration, this fidget game transforms the learning experience into an engaging adventure.

Aligned with the Science of Reading (SoR), The Fidget Game focuses on teaching sight words, also known as Dolch words. These commonly used words are crucial for developing reading fluency and comprehension. The game utilizes a multisensory approach, stimulating multiple senses to enhance learning and retention.

Within the game, children encounter colorful fidget game mats, dice, and packets of sight words. These tactile elements provide a hands-on learning experience, allowing children to physically interact with the words and concepts they are learning. The game fosters a deeper understanding of language mechanics and improves reading fluency.

“The Fidget Game is an innovative educational toy that combines fun and learning. Its multisensory approach engages children and helps them develop essential reading skills.” – Reading Specialist, Jennifer Smith

The Fidget Game serves as a valuable educational toy, promoting both independent and collaborative learning. It encourages children to explore, experiment, and discover the world of reading in an enjoyable and interactive way.
