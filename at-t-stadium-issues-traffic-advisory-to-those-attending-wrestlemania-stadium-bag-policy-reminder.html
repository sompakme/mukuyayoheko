AT&amp;T Stadium Issues Traffic Advisory to Those Attending WrestleMania, Stadium Bag Policy Reminder

July 14, 2024
blog

AT&T Stadium issued the following email this morning to WrestleMania 32 ticket holders:

WrestleMania
Sunday, April 3, 2016
AT&T Stadium

We are excited to have you at AT&T Stadium for WrestleMania 32 on Sunday, April 3rd.  In preparation of the event, we recommend pre-purchasing your parking pass. By pre-purchasing your parking, you save $5 on the price of the pass, guarantee a space in an AT&T Stadium parking lot and can plan your route to the stadium accordingly.

To purchase your parking pass, click here.

Important Traffic Information

  • Please ARRIVE EARLY to avoid traffic and congestion in the Arlington area.
  • Below are two suggested routes to use for arrival.  These alternative routes will allow you to avoid the heavily congested Collins Street, Ballpark Way and AT&T Way exits off of I-30.

I-30 Westbound Preferred Route: Take I-30 Westbound to SH 161; Take SH 161 Southbound to Main Street/Division Street exit; Turn Right on Main Street/Division Street; Turn Right on AT&T Way to Silver Lots or Turn Right on Collins Street to Blue Lots.

I-30 Eastbound Preferred Route: Take I-30 Eastbound to Center Street (Exit 27B); Keep Right on ramp towards Center Street; Turn Right on Center Street; Turn Left on Randol Mill Road to Blue Lots or Turn Left on Division Street and Left on AT&T Way to Silver Lots.

We recommend that you use www.dallascowboysmaps.com for your travels to AT&T Stadium.  This will provide you the best route from your point of origin to your pre-purchased or selected cash parking lot.

The I-30 Express Lane is open and available for use.  Look for the exit signs for the “Express Lane” as you enter Tarrant County on I-30 WB from Dallas and East.

We look forward to seeing you at AT&T Stadium!

Remember: No Purses. No Bags. View AT&T Stadium Purse and Bag Policy here.
