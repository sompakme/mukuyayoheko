Knight has been feuding with Bray Wyatt for several weeks, and they will finally clash at WWE Royal Rumble. The bout is being advertised as “The Mountain Dew Pitch Black Match”, but WWE has yet to offer any explanation as to what the stipulation entails. Knight previously stated he had been left in the dark about the match.

Speaking on WWE After The Bell with Corey Graves, Knight offered an update by noting that he doesn’t know what the match will be, but according to “every indication” he has been given, it will be a Street Fight that’s somewhat in the dark.

“Pitch Black Match, you know, I think it’s something new in the making,” Knight said. “There was something to a point I was I like I don’t know what the hell this is, I don’t know what this is gonna be. But every indication that I’ve been given is it’s gonna be a kickass street fight, it’s gonna basically be kind of in the dark.

“I don’t know what the lighting is gonna be, I don’t know how that’s gonna work. I don’t know what other kind of other factors or elements are gonna come into play. All I know is that it’s gonna be a fight at the Royal Rumble.”

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
