Engagement rumors have been following Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce for months, but the couple have been careful not to comment on the speculation. However, Jason Kelce, Travis’ older brother and fellow NFL star, recently seemed to let a hint slip about the power couple’s wedding plans.

During the Wednesday, May 29 episode of their New Heights podcast, Travis and Jason engaged in a lively discussion about Adam Sandler movies and the characters they most related to.
