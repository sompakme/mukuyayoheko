How do you pronounce Audemar Piguet?

The correct pronunciation of Audemars Piguet is Oh-duh-mahr pee-geh. The “Au” at the beginning of Audemars is pronounced with an “oh” sound that is slightly closed; it is pronounced by pursing your lips while saying “oh”. The following “de” is pronounced with a “duh” sound.
