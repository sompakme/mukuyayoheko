Who Is Craig Heywards Son?

July 28, 2024
blog

Craig Heyward and his wife, Charlotte, had four sons together – Craig, Jr., Cameron, Corey, and Connor, and all of them are professional sportsmen.

Cameron Heyward

Cameron plays football as a defensive tackle for the Pittsburgh Steelers of the NFL.

The Steelers had drafted him in the first round of the 2011 NFL Draft.

Before then, he played college football at Ohio State.

Cameron was born on May 6, 1989. He is married to Allie Schwarzwalder.

Craig Heyward Jr.

Craig Jr. is the eldest amongst the Heyward brothers.

He played football while he was at Passaic high school and later, special teams at Tennessee State.

The Trenton Steel later signed him in as a running back. However, Craig’s career didn’t last long as he quit playing and took up the role of an assistant coach in Nutley High School, New Jersey.

Corey Heyward

Corey was born on August 29, 1993, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States.

He stands 1.85 m tall and currently plays basketball with the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets as a Guard.

Connor Heyward

Connor, Heyward’s youngest son, plays football as a tight end and fullback for the Pittsburgh Steelers of the NFL.

Born on January 22, 1999, in Georgia, Connor played college football at Michigan State.

Connor is 1.83 m tall and weighs 104 kg.
