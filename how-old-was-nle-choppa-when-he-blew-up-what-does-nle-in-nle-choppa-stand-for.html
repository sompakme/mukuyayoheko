How old was NLE Choppa when he blew up? What does NLE in NLE Choppa stand for?

Bryson Lashun Potts known professionally as NLE Choppa (previously YNR Choppa), is an American rapper who hails from Memphis, Tennessee.

NLE Choppa is managed by his mother, Angela Potts, and she has been managing him since he started expressing an interest in rap music.

NLE Choppa released his first song, “No Love Anthem”, in March 2018 under the name YNR Choppa.

On July 22, 2018, NLE Choppa released his debut mixtape, No Love the Takeover.

In December 2018, NLE Choppa appeared on “No Chorus Pt. 3”, a cypher-style song by his Shotta Fam collective.

NLE Choppa’s opening verse and his dance moves in the accompanying music video made him stand out from the group, earning him an online buzz.

How old was NLE Choppa when he blew up?

NLE Choppa blew up at the age of 17 after he released the single “Shotta Flow” in 2019, which was certified platinum by the RIAA and peaked in the top-40 of the Billboard Hot 100. H

NLE Choppa’ debut EP, Cottonwood, was also released in 2019.

What does NLE in NLE Choppa stand for?

The “NLE” in Choppa’s stage name stands for No Love Entertainment and it refers to a movement Choppa launched with some of his friends and local artists in Memphis.
