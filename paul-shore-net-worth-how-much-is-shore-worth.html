Top 5 Pauly Shore Movies

Pauly Shore has appeared in numerous films throughout his career, showcasing his unique comedic talent and leaving a lasting impression on audiences. Here are five of his most notable movies:

2. Bio-Dome

Released in 1996, “Bio-Dome” stars Pauly Shore and Stephen Baldwin as two slacker friends who mistakenly find themselves trapped inside a closed ecological experiment. The film is known for its quirky humor and offbeat premise.

3. Son in Law

In this 1993 comedy, Pauly Shore portrays Crawl, a laid-back college student who poses as the fiancé of a small-town girl (played by Carla Gugino) to help her deal with her conservative family during Thanksgiving break. The film combines fish-out-of-water humor with heartfelt moments.

4. Jury Duty

Released in 1995, “Jury Duty” features Pauly Shore as an aimless slacker who tries to avoid work by faking his way onto a high-profile murder case. As a member of the jury, he creates chaos while attempting to uncover the truth.

5. In the Army Now

In this 1994 comedy, Pauly Shore stars as Bones Conway, a hapless slacker who enlists in the U.S. Army Reserve with his friend. The film follows their misadventures during basic training and their subsequent deployment to a conflict zone.
