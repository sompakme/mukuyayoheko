Early Life

Khing Bassey was born Kingsley Christopher Bassey on November 2. He is from Biquo in Calabar, Cross River.

After his basic education, he obtained a degree in Computer Science at the University of Lagos.

Career

Khing Bassey started acting professionally in 2008. He became known for his role of Charles Enahoro in the TV series Tinsel.

He got exposure for his role in White Hunter staring alongside the likes of Funke Akindele, Mercy Johnson, and Ini Edo.
Ever since he came to the limelight he has been a force to reckon with in the industry, appearing in top films.

He has a starred in other movies such as Thirty Peices of Silver, The Woman I married, The Promise, Our first Born, If tomorrow comes, About Time, Airline Babes, Wounded Harmony, and Love Like a Fire.

He is also the founder of the film production company, Khing C. Bassey (KCB) Productions.
