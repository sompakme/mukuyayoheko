How Taylor And Travis’ Relationship Is Being Impacted By Public Pressure

“She and Travis are now five months into their relationship, and because of all the attention and the scrutiny, they’re really starting to feel the pressure,” the source said. “All eyes are on them, and friends are worried the pair won’t make it.”

This was evident on Taylor’s 34th birthday, last Wednesday. Leading up to the event, there was all sorts of speculation that Travis would go all out. One report claimed he was planning a big party with her friends and family.

However, the singer’s birthday came and went. Taylor celebrated by going out in New York with a slew of famous friends, but Travis was nowhere in sight. Not only did this debunk rumors he was doing something big for her birthday, but it caused speculation that something was amiss between the two, which is evidence of how scrutiny Taylor and Travis’ relationship is under.

“It’s been a whirlwind between her concerts, his football games, and meeting each other’s parents and friends,” the insider continued. “Now that they’re coming down to earth a little, there appears to be trouble ahead.”

The source emphasized that trouble could be brewing ahead. “But the hard conversations are coming up,” they continued. “For one thing, they’ve had to deal with nasty rumors.”

Taylor Doesn’t Believe The Rumors Being Spread About Travis

As excited as many fans are to see Taylor in a new relationship, it hasn’t been without controversy. Travis’ past comment shave been brought up, including references he made to wanting to find a “breeder” – aka. a woman to have children with – during an episode of his podcast that aired earlier this year before their relationship, which some fans have called distasteful.

Travis has also been accused of having a history of infidelity. His ex-girlfriend Kayla Nicole issued a public warning to Taylor when speaking to Daily Mail in the summer. Not only did Kayla say that Travis cheated on her, but she warned the pop singer to be wary of his intentions.
