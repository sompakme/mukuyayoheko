Mention the name Rolex and one will automatically think of luxury watches. Originally established as Wilsdorf and Davis by Hans Wildorf and Alfred Davis in London in 1905, the company eventually changed its name to Rolex and moved to Switzerland in 1919. It is considered as one of the most valuable global brands in the world. It is known to produce a couple of thousand watches each day, with revenues reaching billions of dollars each year.

Rolex has produced several innovations in the timepiece industry. These include the waterproof watch and the automatically changing date watch. It was also the first watch to show two time zones at once and also the first to earn chronometer certification.

The brand is a favorite of not only gentlemen, but also of ladies. Here are the top 10 most expensive watches made by Rolex for women.

The Rolex Ladies Masterpiece White Gold watch has a case that measures 29 millimeters in diameter. Its bezel is set with 116 diamonds and the case lugs are set with 24 diamonds. This automatic watch has an hour, minute, second and date functions. Its case and band are made of 18k white gold. The dress style ladies watch has a blue dial with diamonds. Its market price is $45,150.
