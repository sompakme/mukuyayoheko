The journey of every actor is unique, and it could take a performer years to make it in Hollywood. However, once someone takes off, there is simply no stopping them. This is exactly what we have seen from Pedro Pascal, who took some time to become a household name.

Pascal is brilliant in the Star Wars realm on The Mandalorian, and he will be starring on The Last of Us in 2023. Video game adaptations have had mixed success, but the network believes that Pascal can turn the project into a hit. In fact, they are shelling out huge money for Pascal's expertise.

Let's take a look at how much Pedro Pascal will be making for The Last of Us.

How Much Is Pedro Pascal Making For 'The Last Of Us'?

Pedro Pascal is one of the most likable guys working in entertainment today, and after years of putting in great work, he has become one of the most popular actors around.

Pascal has shown a penchant for thriving on both the big and small screen during his years in acting.

On the big screen, Pascal has been in movies like Kingsman: The Golden Circle, The Equalizer 2, Wonder Woman 1984, and he will be appearing in The Unbearable Weight of Massive Talent with Nicolas Cage. This is all great, but Pascal has made even bigger waves on TV.

While making a name for himself on TV, Pascal has been in some truly amazing projects. The actor has appeared on shows like Buffy the Vampire Slayer, NYPD Blue, Law & Order, The Good Wife, CSI, Graceland, and Game of Thrones.

As a leading man, Pascal's work on both Narcos and The Mandalorian have been exceptional, and those shows proved that he could lead a series to success.

Pascal has been doing some amazing work, and while people are excited to see the next season of The Mandalorian, the actor has another upcoming project that has been generating a considerable amount of buzz.

'The Last Of Us' Is Based On A Video Game Series

In February 2021, it was announced that Pedro Pascal was going to be taking the lead on The Last of Us, a series based on the smash-hit video game franchise.

"Based on the critically acclaimed video game The Last of Us, developed by Naughty Dog exclusively for the PlayStation platforms, the story takes place twenty years after modern civilization has been destroyed. Joel (Pascal), a hardened survivor, is hired to smuggle Ellie (Game of Thrones‘ Bella Ramsey), a 14-year-old girl, out of an oppressive quarantine zone. What starts as a small job soon becomes a brutal, heartbreaking journey, as they both must traverse across the U.S. and depend on each other for survival," Deadline summarized about the show.

As you can imagine, the hype is through the roof for this show. Video game adaptations have been getting better, and turning this franchise into a series is easily the best route that could have been taken.

Pascal is going to be brilliant on the show, and the network is paying a fortune to get the best of the actor on screen.

Pascal Will Make A Fortune On The Project

So, how much is the talented Pedro Pascal going to make for his starring role on the upcoming Last of Us series? Well, thanks to having plenty of success on television prior to landing the role, Pascal is going to be making a fortune on the show.

According to The Gamer, via Variety, the actor "will earn $6 million for playing Joel during The Last Of Us's first season. Since it has already been confirmed that the show's first season will be ten episodes long, that works out at $600,000 per episode. That figure is apparently before tax, union fees, and anything else that has to come out of it, but it still means Pascal will be taking home a lot of money for his work."

It is pretty rare for an actor to start off his time on a series making this type of money, but Pedro Pascal brings a significant amount of name value to the project. Not only that, but the series should receive a substantial boost from having a built-in audience thanks to the success of the video game franchise. These two factors have certainly played a part in Pascal's huge salary.
