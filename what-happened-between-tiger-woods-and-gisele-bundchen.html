Tom Brady and Tiger Woods are known to be good friends, but their friendship could be on the rocks for a surprising reason. Woods recently broke up with his ex-girlfriend Erica Herman, and fans think that his next partner is likely to be Brady's ex-wife Gisele Bundchen.

BetOnline statistics put Bundchen ahead of names like Kim Kardashian, Shakira, Eva Longoria, Vera Wang, Charlize Theron, Katie Holmes, Monique Samuels and Jada Pinkett Smith. This comes after his complicated break-up with his ex-girlfriend Erica Herman that led to a $30 million dollar lawsuit against Woods.

Here's everything that you need to know about Wood's relationships.

Tiger Woods's Relationship With Erica Herman

Tiger Woods and Erica Herman were together for 6 years before their breakup in 2022. Herman is currently suing Woods because, according to her, he kicked her out of their shared home using trickery. The court documents read, "Specifically, by trickery, agents of Woods convinced Herman to pack a suitcase for a short vacation and, when she arrived at the airport, theytold her she had been locked out of her residence, in violation of the oral tenancy agreement and in violation of Florida law"

She also filed a separate lawsuit attempting to nullify a NDA she signed at the beginning of the relationship. She says that the NDA has left her questioning what areas of her life she can share with others.

"Because of the aggressive use of the Woods NDA against her by the Defendant and the trust under his control, the Plaintiff is unsure whether she may disclose, among other things, facts giving rise to various legal claims she believes she has"

Tiger Wood's Trust has denied the existence of an oral tenacity agreement. "In reality, Ms. Herman was invited to live in the Residence while she was in a relationship with her former boyfriend. After Mr. Woods recently terminated the relationship, Ms. Herman was advised that she was no longer welcome in the Residence."

The pair was introduced to each other by Jesper Parnevik, who regrets setting them up. Tragically enough, it was revealed that Nordegen initially had doubts about dating Woods.
