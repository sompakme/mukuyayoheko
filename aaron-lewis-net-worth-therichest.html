Being the lead vocalist and guitarist of the band Staind, Aaron Lewis has an estimated net worth of $9 million. He was born in Rutland, Vermont but he spent almost all of his childhood life in Longmeadow, Massachusetts where he got musically influenced by his musician parents. When he was young, he listens to songs by CSNY and James Taylor. His trademark tone of singing was inspired by the 90s "Seattle scene".

Staind has released a total of seven albums. The first of which was titled as "Tormented". But his commercial debut only came after "Outside", a live collaboration of him and Limp Bizkit's vocalist Fred Durst in the Family Values Tour in the year 1999.

In addition to his band activities, he also plays solo acoustic music shows ever since he was seventeen years old up to the present.

When Lewis has free time, he loves to get together with his family of three, go hunting or play golf.
