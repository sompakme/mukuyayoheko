Gigi Hadid Wears Her Jeans Inside-Out: Love It or Hate It?

September 29, 2024
blog

Well, this is a new way to wear your jeans!

Gigi Hadid, who keeps showing Us innovative new ways to wear clothing, just pulled a risky move with her denim.

The Maybelline spokeswoman, 20, was a guest judge on a season 8 episode of RuPaul’s Drag Race on April 4 on Logo, and wore a pair of inside-out boyfriend-fit jeans.

The look featured exposed muslin pockets, seam stitching and rolled cuffs. Though it is clear that her jeans were made that way (the belt loops, fly and buttons are all facing outward, so she didn’t just pull on a pair of jeans the wrong way), it’s still an intense outfit.

Hadid, who is the daughter of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Yolanda Foster, didn’t stop the head-turning look there. She also paired the out-there pants with a black bandeau, a neon orange cropped jacket and blue suede pumps.

Her Drag Race outfits got even more colorful from there. Taking a pic from her dressing room, Hadid shared another mixed-hue ensemble: a pastel bomber jacket and matching high-waisted pants with a teal bralette.

“MONDAY NIGHT. @RUPAULSDRAGRACE. I’m judging SNATCH GAME!! @logotv 9pm @rupaulofficial,” she wrote.
