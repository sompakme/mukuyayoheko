True Thompson is The Miracle to Khloe’s Life

True Thompson came to Khloe’s life as a miracle after she had endured years of infertility so it is not surprising that Khloe makes sure that she treats her like one. Khloe reportedly went on a long and painful road to motherhood before she finally welcomed her first child. She was even so afraid that her pregnancy might not push through so she waited about six months into her pregnancy before she finally announced it on her social media.

With the hardships she went through to finally get to have a child like True, she makes sure that no expense is spared to spoil her lovely daughter. She showers her with expensive luxury designer clothes and made sure she is living like a princess which is why they are now living in a $37 million mansion. She was even seen letting her daughter wear a $900 Fendi boots and $1,570 Louis Vuitton purse which is a waste of money for some people, but for Khloe, it is just her way of showering True some love.

Luxury Celebrations and Gifts

Once, Khloe posted a Snapchat story where a three month old True is seen wearing a diamond earrings. As early as seven months old, True has been bombarded with luxury items as gifts. Khloe posted on her Instagram account a picture of True where the baby was seen giggling from inside an expensive Hermès Birkin bag. The bag is notoriously pricey and Khloe does not care if True might damage the goods, as long as True is happy. True was also seen in another post where she sits in a sea of about a dozen of luxury bags which is estimated to be valued around $154,000 minimum. Seeing the beautiful girl True playing with a Birkin bag these days is no longer surprising especially to the fans who follow the Kardashians on social media.

Today, True is seen as a little fashionista dressed in luxurious dresses and bags. Khloe and True loves twinning with their outfit covered with matching luxury branded clothes and bags. The duo has matched outfit numerous times and it is always with designer pieces together. True is growing up with the most luxurious wardrobe. The luxury does not even stop there since Khloe makes sure that every celebration in True’s life is extra. For True’s third birthday last year, Khloe’s daughter was dressed like a princess and she’s surrounded with beautiful pink balloons. Upon arrival, the guests of True are greeted with Disney princess actresses waiting for them at the door.

Extravagant Vacations

While growing up with luxurious branded items, True is also growing up loving some extravagant vacations. Khloe loves sharing pictures of their vacation where True is seen enjoying each time. This is not something any three years old can experience. These luxurious beach getaways in True's life started as early as four months old, True has been seen having some actions at the beach. Her parents loved to take her to the beach to enjoy sands and the sun. Through the years, the world has seen True’s adventures and vacations as uploaded on her mom’s social media accounts.

Although Khloe has showered True with luxurious lifestyle, she makes sure that she spends some quality time with True.
