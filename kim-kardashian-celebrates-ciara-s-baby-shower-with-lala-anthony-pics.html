Kim Kardashian Celebrates Ciara's Baby Shower With LaLa Anthony: Pics

August 09, 2024
blog

Kim Kardashian is getting majorly nostalgic! More than 9 months after giving birth to daughter North West, the significantly slimmed down star happily celebrated pal Ciara‘s impending bundle of joy at her baby shower on March 22. 

Posting pictures of the singer’s festivities on Instagram, Kardashian, 33, posed with Ciara and friend LaLa Anthony at the outdoor bash. 

The reality star wore a white cutout dress with a thigh-high leg slit and minimal jewelry. Ciara, 28, looked stunning in a loose white, floor-length Naeem Khan dress with vibrant red embroidery down the middle, and Anthony, 34, wore a flirty printed halter sundress. 

“Celebrating @Ciara’s baby shower today with @lala #mommymemories,” Kardashian captioned the picture. 

North’s mommy has spent plenty of time with the “Get Up” singer during her pregnancy. The pair were spotted shopping for baby clothes at Bel Bambini in February. Ciara announced her first pregnancy with rapper Future on The View in January and has since appeared on the Grammys red carpet. 

Kim’s mom Kris Jenner also attended the baby shower, snapping a photo with Ciara.

“Mamma Kris In The Building! #SelfieFun:),” Ciara captioned the picture.
