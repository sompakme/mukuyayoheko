Bryan Tanaka (born April 11, 1983) is an American Master Choreographer, Teacher, Performer, and Social Media Personality from Lacey, Washington. In April 2020, he garnered immense media attention after coming out as a lover of Mariah Carey (American singer and record producer).

She also works as a songwriter, actress, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. Mariah first became famous after releasing her eponymous debut album in 1990. Furthermore, she is dubbed as the “Songbird Supreme” by Guinness World Records.

Her debut remained on the first position on the U.S. Billboard 200 for eleven consecutive weeks. In fact, she also became the only singer whose five songs bagged number one rank on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Some of her other popular projects are “Merry Christmas”, “Daydream”, “Butterfly”, “Music Box”, “Glitter”, “Precious”, “The Emancipation of Mimi”, etc. In her successful career, Mariah won many awards including five Grammy Awards.
