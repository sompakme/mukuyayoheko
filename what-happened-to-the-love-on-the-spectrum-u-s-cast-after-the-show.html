What Happened To The 'Love On The Spectrum U.S.' Cast After The Show?

July 18, 2024
blog

Netflix's Love On The Spectrum premiered in 2019. The Australian reality series was nothing like anyone had seen, and it quickly made the country's top ten list shortly after its release. Over the years, there have been strange reality dating shows, but the concept of this one is unique. The premier show featured a cast of nine adults on the Autism spectrum as they navigate the world of dating in search of a romantic partner. The success of the Australian show led to the launch of its stateside version, Love on the Spectrum U.S.

Being one of Netflix's new dating shows, the American version features a cast of six adults on the spectrum in their quest to find love. Finding love on a dating show is tricky and being on the spectrum makes it more challenging. Luckily for the cast members, they didn't have to walk the path alone, as autism expert Jennifer Cook held their hands through it all. Since its premiere, the show has gathered buzz online, leaving viewers to puzzle about what happened to the Love on the Spectrum U.S. cast after the show.

8 James Hasn't Given Up On Looking For Love

James appeared on the first season of the Netflix dating docuseries, hoping to find a true life partner. The 34-year-old Renaissance Faire enthusiast came close to fulfilling this need with co-star Emma. The two went on seemingly great dates, but hopes of progressing into romantic partners were cut short when Emma opted for friendship instead.

Currently, he lives with his parents and hangs out with friends when he is not engaged with work. James is still searching for love and has employed both online and in-person means for this cause, as finding love is important to him.

7 Abbey Is 24 And In Love

The Madeby Abbey owner celebrated her 24th birthday weeks ago. Abbey was one lucky few to find love on the Love On The Spectrum U.S. The hatmaker is enjoying a blossoming relationship with her boyfriend, David, whom she began a relationship with on the show. Abbey appears to be having the time of her life, creating videos and sharing pictures on TikTok and Instagram, where she is primarily active.

6 Kaelynn's DMs Are Getting Flooded

Kaelynn and cast member Peter were one of the show's favorite pairs. Fans of the dating show had high hopes during the pair's speed dating session, but all that came crashing when Peter canceled a second meeting. Since filming, Kaelynn has been vocal on her Instagram page, sharing stories about her interests. Although unable to find a partner on the show, the autism advocate has had numerous proposals from people seeking to be her partner.

5 Dani Is Still On The Hunt For Love

Dani and cast member Solomon gave viewers the mushiest date moments on the show. The budding romance came to an end barely two months after filming. Although things did not work out as Dani envisioned, she learned the importance of setting boundaries from the beginning. These days, the Danimation founder is a busy bee; she runs a significant business while actively pursuing a Ph.D. Dani's search for love has been strenuous, but she's optimistic that it will happen soon.

4 Steve Is Still In Search Of A 'Lovely Lady'

Steve joined the show in search of a 'lovely lady.' While he didn't get that, his calm nature did leave a lasting impression on the viewers. In an interview with Netflix, the 63-year-old revealed that he has been engaging in more spiritually rewarding activities in the synagogue. The sexagenarian is still searching for a partner, and this time, he's exploring the option of meeting new people online.

3 Subodh Garg Is In Love

Not everyone is lucky enough to find love on a dating show, but Subdoh managed to cop the love of his life on the show despite the odds. From its looks, Subodh is head over heels in love with Racheal, and his latest Instagram post is proof of it. 2 David Is Exploring TikTok

David joined the video-sharing platform a few days ago, and he has been on a roll with creating content. David and his Abbey formed a strong connection that progressed from friendship to relationship on the show. The bond between the couple appears to be solid, as David has caught the TikTok bug from his girlfriend, Abbey, who is known to be active on the platform. David will be sharing nuggets on being a great boyfriend, recipes, and lifestyle videos as per his posted content.

1 Rachel Is Keeping A Low Profile

Rachel Osterbach is not a stranger to reality T.V.; she was a cast member of A&E's docuseries Born This Way. After going on three dates, the motivational speaker agreed to be Subodh's girlfriend, and the lovebirds are kicking their relationship to newer levels. Although Rachel has a Twitter and Instagram presence, the reality T.V. star rarely updates her social media pages. However, snippets of the autism advocate having a good time are regularly seen on her boyfriend's Instagram stories.
