But hey, big bucks buy big wins, and the Chiefs are walking proof. Their roster boasts some of the league's highest-paid players, each one a crucial cog in the championship machine.

Think Patrick Mahomes, the golden-armed quarterback slinging passes worth $45 million, or Chris Jones, the defensive wrecking ball earning $19.5 million to dismantle offensive lines.

The Kansas City Chiefs' swift ascent to Super Bowl glory within four years and multiple conference final appearances underscore their status as a powerhouse in the league — a team poised to dominate for years to come.

10 George Karlaftis

George Karlaftis' Salary - $3,255,129

George Karlaftis was selected 30th overall in the 2022 NFL Draft, bringing his talents from Purdue University.

Securing his future with a four-year, $11.9 million contract, Karlaftis boasts an average annual salary of $3 million. While not yet ranking among the Chiefs' top earners, his rookie year showed flashes of potential, and his contract reflects the belief he holds.

His rookie season saw him earn a base salary of $1,247,522 with a $1,465,086 signing bonus, and for 2024, his salary jumps to $1,790,043.

While his stats were not eye-popping in his first year, registering four sacks and 13 tackles, his rookie struggles are often expected for defensive ends adapting to the pro level.

But his raw talent and explosiveness on the field have Chiefs fans excited.

Looking ahead, Karlaftis' contract holds a fifth-year option, giving the Chiefs a chance to extend his stay if he continues to develop.

8 Harrison Butker

Harrison Butker's Salary - $4,568,796

Earning the trust of Chiefs fans with clutch kicks is not cheap, and Harrison Butker, the team's kicker since 2017, knows that well.

Drafted in the sixth round of the 2017 NFL Draft out of Georgia Tech, Butker inked a five-year, $20.3 million contract extension in 2019, securing his spot as the Chiefs' primary point-getter.

This translates to a handsome average annual salary of $4,055,000, placing him firmly in the middle of the Chiefs' salary rankings.

With a base salary of $3,690,000 in 2024 and a potential workout bonus of $50,000, Butker's value extends beyond his base contract.

His reliability and clutch performances, like the game-winner against the Buffalo Bills in the 2022 AFC Divisional Round, solidify his worth.

Butker's success is not solely measured in dollars and cents. He holds the franchise record for the longest field goal (58 yards) and boasts a perfect postseason record in Super Bowl LIII.

Despite some inconsistencies, his consistent clutch performances make him an invaluable asset for the Chiefs. So, while his salary may not rival the Chiefs' top earners, his impact on the field (and in fans' hearts) goes far beyond the price tag.

1 Patrick Mahomes

Patrick Mahomes' Salary - $45,000,000

As the Kansas City Chiefs' standout player and a top NFL star, Patrick Mahomes has earned fame and a $70 million net worth through his NFL salary, sponsorships, and other ventures.

In 2017, Mahomes became the highest-paid player in the league when he signed a modest four-year deal with the Kansas City Chiefs worth $16.2 million, including a fully guaranteed $10 million signing bonus.

After quickly establishing himself as a superstar quarterback, speculation swirled about the potential magnitude of his next contract, with many analysts predicting it would rewrite NFL records.

On July 6, 2020, Mahomes shattered expectations by signing a groundbreaking ten-year extension with the Chiefs, initially reported to be over $400 million.

Subsequent reports revealed the deal's total value to be an astounding $503 million, making Mahomes the first half-billion dollar athlete in the history of professional athletics.

His contract includes $477 million in guaranteed mechanisms, with $140 million fully guaranteed against injury. Beyond his on-field earnings, Mahomes has secured numerous lucrative endorsement deals.

In August 2020, Mahomes further expanded his financial portfolio by acquiring an ownership stake in the Kansas City Royals baseball team.
