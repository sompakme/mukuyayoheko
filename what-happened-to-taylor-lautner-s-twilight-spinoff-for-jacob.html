What Happened To Taylor Lautner's Twilight Spinoff For Jacob?

August 30, 2024
blog

The Twilight franchise was a colossal hit years back, and while those films weren't considered great by some, they were still massively successful, and they turned its stars into household names.

Taylor Lautner's time in the Twilight franchise was lucrative, and gave him a solid net worth, as well as international celebrity status.

At one point, it sure looked like Lautner was going to possibly get a spin-off movie in the Twilight franchise, and it could have been a huge success. However, that movie never came to pass, nor did the proposed book series.

Let's take a look back on what happened to that spin-off.

It Took A Couple Of Years For Taylor Lautner To Establish Himself

During the early part of the 2000s, Taylor Lautner made his way into the entertainment industry looking to become a successful performer. It would take some time, but eventually, Lautner was able to carve out a nice career for himself.

After being featured in some film and television projects, and early win for the star came in the movie Sharkboy and Lava Girl. Though it was not a huge box office success, it was one of those movies that virtually everyone saw once it hit Blockbuster Video.

This helped set the stage for the performer to start landing bigger and better roles that allowed him to showcase his skills.

These days, Lautner is not a prolific performer, per se, but he still takes on the occasional role, something that fans of his are always excited to see.

Lautner has had many notable projects during his time in Hollywood, but to this day, his time in the Twilight franchise remains his biggest success.

Taylor Lautner Saw A Huge Shift In His Career Thanks To Jacob Black And The 'Twilight' Franchise

If you weren't around to see it, then it's hard to imagine the hold that Twilight had on the world. The story itself was a sensation in the pages, and once it hit the big screen, vampires were back in action.

Taylor Lautner was cast as Jacob Black, one of the main character's of the story. This turned Lautner into a household name, and women everywhere were fawning over the performer while the saga was hitting the big screen.

Lautner was physically jacked for the role, but it came at a heavy price.

"The body was great for the role—but the amount of the working out and discipline and food that went into it, not fond memories. It's tough because when I was 17, 18, 19 years old, I couldn't do any cardio because I had to just put on as much muscle as possible," he told E! News.

Though it was tough, Lautner pulled it off, and cemented his place in the hearts of fans everywhere.

Jacob Black remains a character that people love, and years back, Lautner had dished on the possibility of the character's story continuing.

Taylor Lautner Hinted At A New Twilight Film In 2013

"Taylor Lautner hinted that there may well be the option to spin out Jacob and Renesmee’s love story into a new Twilight film. Marie Claire met with the star on his recent trip to London and asked if we’d be seeing more of his romance with Edward and Bella’s daughter," Marie Claire reported.

This gave way to a response from Lautner that got fans worked up.

"I could see potential, I mean it [Breaking Dawn Part 2] doesn’t really explore it, although it does have a nice close to it," he said.

Twilight fans would have loved nothing more than to see the story continue, and in 2011, it was announced that Stephanie Meyer was working on a story based on Black and Renesmee.

"Stephenie Meyer, creator of the novel series which the popular films are based on, has hinted that she is writing a new Twilight spin-off series that will focus on Jacob Black (Taylor Lautner) and his relationship with Renesmee (Mackenzie Foy), the daughter of Bella Swan (Kristen Stewart) and Edward Cullen (Robert Pattinson), whom Jacob 'imprints' upon at the time of birth, marking the newborn as his life mate (or life protector, however you want to look at it)," Business Insider reported.

All these years later, and nothing has materialized.

In 2020, author Stephanie Meyers did release Midnight Sun, which was the story from Edward's perspective, but it was not necessarily a continuation of the story that many fans had grown up with.
