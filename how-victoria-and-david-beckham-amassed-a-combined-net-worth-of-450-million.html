How Victoria And David Beckham Amassed A Combined Net Worth Of $450 Million

Highlights

  • David Beckham's career as a soccer player and his smart partnership deals have contributed to his massive net worth of $450 million.
  • Victoria Beckham's successful music career with the Spice Girls and her fashion and cosmetics line have also played a significant role in their combined wealth.
  • Investments, such as David's purchase of Inter Miami, have further increased their net worth, with the soccer club now valued at over $1 billion after signing Lionel Messi.

Thanks to their respective careers, investments, and smart partnership deals, David Beckham and Victoria Beckham's combined net worth has reached $450 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Those numbers, while staggering, are only likely to increase in the coming years.

David made most of his fortune from his heyday as one of the best soccer players in Britain, before he went on to sign an astounding $250 million deal with the LA Galaxy of the MLS, which saw him relocate to the U.S. with his family in 2007.

Victoria, on the other hand, was pulling in tens of millions a year from her booming music career as part of the '90s girl band, Spice Girls. To this day, the mother-of-four, who married David in 1999, makes a substantial amount from merchandise and record sales while also juggling her fashion and cosmetics line.

The duo has fronted some of the biggest endorsement deals in Hollywood, with David famously getting paid $20 million to take on the position as Giorgio Armani's global ambassador. But what other ways have seen David and Victoria's net worth reach $450 million? Here's the lowdown.

David has retired from playing soccer himself, but he now owns a majority of Inter Miami, an MLS expansion franchise, which made its debut in March 2020.
