What Led To Steve Howey And Sarah Shahi’s Divorce?

Of course, it should go without saying that the only people who truly know what led to Sarah Shahi and Steve Howey’s divorce are the people who are directly involved. “My wife and I were like any other couple. All of our time was for each other. We would go on vacation, we’d have ice cream, we’d go to a movie, we’d do whatever we wanted to do. Because it was hard and we weren’t used to it.”
