His net worth is currently estimated to be around $250 million dollars and his car collection is worth almost $1 million. Let’s take a look at what makes up his luxurious garage.

2008 Hummer H3 ($15,000)

The 2008 Hummer H3 worth $15,000 was first used for military purposes before the civilian versions were produced. This brought about a phenomenon called “the Hummer brand cult following”. Justin’s Hummer H3 SUV was introduced in 2006 and unlike the H1 and H2 models, the H3 was not developed by AM General. The H3 has a power supply from a 5.3-liter V8 engine that generates 220 hp and 305 Nm torque

This car with a 1.8-liter, four-cylinder turbo petrol engine is reported to be Justin Timberlake’s daily favorite and it boasts 148 hp and 210 Nm torque. It is worth about $26,000 and rated 5/5 stars in passenger and driver protection in a frontal impact. The 2002 Volkswagen Jetta has also received four stars for side-impact testing from the NHTSA.

2013 Jeep Wrangler Rubicon ($28,000)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Just like the 2008 Hummer H3, the 2013 Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is another military-derived jeep in Justin Timberlake’s luxurious garage. Justin’s Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is actually an off-road-ready version of the common Jeep Wrangler and it proffers 32In BF Goodrich Mud-Terrain off-road tires with militant tread, 17In alloy wheels and a four-wheel drive with a ratio of 4:1 transfer case and a lot of other features as its standard. In the year 2013, the Jeep got a minor makeover with improvements like a second windshield washer nozzle and cozy and comfortable cabin lighting.

1998 Mercedes-Benz ML320 (W163) ($38,000)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Of course, Justine also owns a 1998 Mercedes ML320 which was initially planned to reinstate the G-class before the idea was later dropped. His 1998 Mercedes ML320 features a 3199cc V6 engine that generates 215 hp and 310 Nm torque. The Mercedes boasts 100 km per hour in less than 10 seconds and a speed of over 160 km per hour. It won the World Car Award in 1999 and its adapted version was used in the 1997 movie “The Lost World: Jurassic Park”.

1993 Acura Legend ($40,000)

The 1993 Acura Legend is a Second-Generation Acura that was first brought to the Acura showroom in 1993. It is a car Justin acquired in the same year he joined the cast of The All-New Mickey Mouse Club as a Mouseketeer and he later got another Acura Legend in 1997 right after he got his driver’s license. The Acura Legend is worth $38,000 and it boasts a 3.6-liter V6 engine that powers 230 HP.

2013 Jeep Grand Cherokee SRT8 ($55,000)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Via Wikimedia Commons
