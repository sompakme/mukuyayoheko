Kehlani has been hard at work in the gym to achieve their current physique, which is nothing short of impressive. In new Instagram photos, the Bay Area beauty showcases their rock-hard abs that look so chiseled, some have accused the star of going under the knife for the look.

“Looks like an etching to me, arms aren’t defined.. legs not defined .. but go ahead n front like the rest of them do,” wrote one IG user within TNT‘s comment section, with another adding, “I don’t like her for this….. is her stomach man made?”

“Lipo abs” added another critic before the “Mind Your Business” singer took to the platform’s comment section to clear the air on rumors of ab etching, a form of lipo that strategically removes fat from the midsection to enhance the look of abs.

“ab etching is absolutely insane when i literally have posted in the gym everyday for the past TWO YEARS and in the past months with an entire weighted vest on,” they clapped back. “when did i have time for surgery and i have an umbilical hernia i can’t even have abdominal surgery please,” they added before dropping eight crying emojis.

They also shared a snap of themselves working out in the weighted vest to further hush those who don’t believe their abs are gym-made.

Many fans took the star at their word while others who actually follow the singer confirmed that their taunt tummy was the result of hard work.

“They post themselves in the the gym every damn day with a weight vest on…you other ppl might be lazy and wanna just lay on the table! but NOT kehlani,” wrote one supporter, with another adding, “this girl really been in the gym and y’all talkin ’bout surgeries.”
